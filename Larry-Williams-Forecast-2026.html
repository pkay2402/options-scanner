Key Lessons on Cycle Analysis

Critical Understanding: Cycles don't forecast magnitude, just timing. The "wave within the cycle" shows probability of rallies/declines. Visual aspects can be deceiving - must measure what actually happened in each wave historically.

Important Cycle Principles:

  • No cycle is correct 100% of the time
  • Sine wave analysis has limitations (assumes same amplitude)
  • Must look at wave-by-wave probability of price rallying
  • Economics/valuations/sentiment affect magnitude
  • Use cycles for timing, not guaranteed predictions
  • Last year's 75% accuracy on wave predictions is excellent odds
