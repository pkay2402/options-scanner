Strategic Investment Opportunities

Welcome to Citrini Research's 26 Trades for 2026 - a thematic watchlist covering the year ahead. This isn't a traditional outlook with rigid forecasts. Instead, it's a comprehensive exploration of potential opportunities across AI infrastructure, geopolitics, consumer trends, macro plays, and speculative moonshots.

From "Bullshit Jobs" benefiting from AI-driven efficiency to "Orbital Manufacturing" moonshots, these ideas span the spectrum from contrarian value plays to high-conviction growth themes. Some are preparation for specific scenarios, others are meant to stay on your radar throughout 2026.
